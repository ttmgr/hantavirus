    Hantaviruses are RNA viruses transmitted via aerosolized rodent excreta. Two syndromes: HPS (Americas) and HFRS (Europe/Asia).

    ANDV (South America, CFR 30-40%, only human-to-human) · SNV (North America, CFR ~36%) · PUUV (Europe, CFR <1%) · HTNV (East Asia, CFR 5-15%).

    Transmission: rodent excreta inhalation. ANDV exceptionally via close human contact. Incubation 1-5 weeks. No approved vaccine. Prevention: rodent control, PPE, building sealing.
